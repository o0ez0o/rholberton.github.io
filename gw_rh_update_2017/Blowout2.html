Blow Out

dye sublimation prints on aluminum
52 prints, 20" x 16" each
2015
edition of 4

These prints revisit a series of photographs of smashed test tubes shot in 2006 and originally printed on archival paper. The rich dye sublimation process on aluminum creates a black mirror, and the shattered glass seems to float in the middle distance.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
set of six
