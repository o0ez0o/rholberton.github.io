Pool

video installation with water,
sound & custom electronics
10' l x 5' w x ~15' h, 20 min. loop
2017

Pool is a single-channel video collage of a collection of tide pools, projected onto the floor from a projector high in the ceiling. Just below the projector is a large dish of water, intermittently stirred by small motors. The motors are triggered by two sound tracks: seagulls and fog horns. The intimate details of tide pool life – shot in HD with a macro lens underwater – are intermittently disturbed by the rippling water, evoking both the fragility and the resiliency of these miniature worlds at the border of sea and land.

Curator and arts writer Marcia Tanner wrote in a review, “Surrounding ‘windows’ give us the crab’s-eye view: a rocky seaweed-garden habitat, flushed continually by the ocean’s ebbs and flows. These intimate glimpses of a vibrant ecosystem teeming with life, full of color and fine detail normally invisible to us, are accompanied by intermittent sea gull cries and the low moans of foghorns. These mournful alarms — one natural, the other human-made — trigger wave-like disturbances… Celebratory yet elegiac, Pool is a poetic meditation on the fragility and resilience of our planet in this time of perilous environmental challenge. With a running time of 20-plus minutes, Pool repays sustained attention.”
